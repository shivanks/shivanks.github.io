About Me

I am a Lead Platform Engineer with over 9 years of experience designing, evolving, and operating large-scale cloud platforms across highly regulated and enterprise environments.

My focus is building platform capabilities that enable teams to move faster while maintaining strong guarantees around reliability, security, and compliance — across architecture, hands-on delivery, and technical leadership.

At Thoughtworks, I work as a Lead Infrastructure Consultant, helping organizations modernize platforms, define cloud strategy, and adopt sustainable DevOps and platform engineering practices.

Previously at Barclays (EDP), I built Kubernetes-based internal platforms for data scientists and engineers, focused on CI/CD, governance, and regulatory compliance in a global banking environment.

I have also worked at Rackspace Technology and Amazon Appstore, improving reliability, automation, and developer workflows across cloud-native systems.
